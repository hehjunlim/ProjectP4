Quick Tips on How to Shop:

  • When going to the dressing room, bring multiple sizes so that you do not have to move back and forth.

  • Try to see the clothing on in front of multiple mirrors in different lighting. Some stores have mirrors that are slightly warped in dressing rooms.

  • Shopping together with someone may prevent you from purchasing quality pieces that you will truly treasure. Their own personal style should not influence yours.

  • Avoid purchasing a clothing piece just because the price is right(example: a shirt is on sale for half of the original price, but the color is not your favorite).

  • If there is an expensive style of clothing you want to buy, test-drive it with a budget friendly option first.

  • Make sure to check the return policy, especially with big purchases at department stores.

  • Online retailers may offer a larger selection of styles and color. However, it is recommended to only purchase items that you are confident about, such as loose t-shirts. Jeans or tight-fitting tops should be tried on in-person since they are riskier.

  • Before going online or in-person shopping, look up coupon codes and shipping promos on various websites.
